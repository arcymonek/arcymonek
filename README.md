- 👋 Hi, I’m @arcymonek, my name is Arkadiusz Polak :)

- 👀 **I’m not a professional programmer**, but I’m passionate about digital sovereignty.\
  I believe that in an era dominated by big cloud providers, building your own infrastructure is the best way to regain control over your own data and privacy.
  
- 🌱 **What do I do?**\
  I’m building and developing my own Home Lab.\
  Thanks to the support of modern language models (LLMs), which I treat as partners in learning, I’ve managed to go from scratch to a fully containerized, secure server environment.
  
- 💞️ **I’m constantly learning new and fascinating things!**

  Self-Hosting:\
    Managing my own services (from media to work tools).
  
  AI & RAG:\
    I’m experimenting with integrating local and cloud-based AI models to boost my personal productivity.\
    I’m currently using LibreChat and various LLMs via their APIs. I’m also exploring third-party tools that extend LibreChat’s capabilities.\
  Security:\
    I’m learning about system hardening, monitoring, and automation (CrowdSec, Nginx, Docker), as well as backups.
  
  Open Source:\
    I try to use open-source solutions wherever I can implement them.\
    And out of gratitude to the developers and the community, I encourage others to do the same. 💞️
  
- 📫 **I’m not looking for the quickest route; I’m seeking a full understanding of what I’m building.**\
  Every challenge is a lesson for me, and every problem solved is another step toward independence. \
  If you’re working on similar topics, I’d love to exchange experiences :)

Greetings from Poland!
